Questions?

Is this legal? It's public record data.

Yes. WA L&I publishes contractor license data as public record. Reselling compiled public data for recruitment purposes is legal in Washington state. We don't scrape — we use their official data portal.

What if there are zero new licenses one week?

You'll still get the email (empty list) so you know the feed is working. Some weeks are slower. Over 90 days, WA averages ~12 new HVAC licenses/month.

Do you have Texas (TDLR) or California (CSLB) data?

Not yet — WA is the pilot. Pro subscribers get early access when we launch TX and CA. The agent architecture is identical; just need to map each state's specialty codes.

Can I get email addresses too?

L&I doesn't publish emails. Pro tier includes phone verification and we provide outreach templates that get replies. Most owners answer their business phone.
